Message Of The Day

  • Be in the Know! Opportunities for all Brock Students

    There is so much happening at Brock despite Covid-19. The best way to find out what’s being offered by faculties, services, and clubs is to explore Experience BU — Brock’s involvement portal and events calendar https://experiencebu.brocku.ca/

    Did you know that there are 89 events listed for this coming week alone? Learn more about Experience BU from Keisha, a third-year Brock student majoring in Biology and Psychology

    Please Update your Contact Information

    Students are asked to ensure their contact information is up-to-date to aid in contact tracing efforts at Brock University in partnership with public health professionals.

    You can read more about Brock University's contact tracing efforts at: brocku.ca/coronavirus/faq.

    Students can update their contact information by accessing the Student Profile page in BrockDB. The second tab along the top of the page labeled Address & Contact will allow you to update your Mailing Address, Local Address, and Alternative Contact information if necessary. Please ensure all of these areas are up-to-date.

    Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for Isaak-Sakai

    Faculty, staff and students are encouraged to enroll in MFA now by following the instructions on the Information Technology Services Website. MFA is now required to login into select Brock University applications including Isaak-Sakai. Problems with Multi-Factor Authentication should be directed to the ITS HelpDesk

    OneDrive will now be Brock students’ main internal storage for files

    On Feb. 12, 2021 ITS will be removing the on-prem student file shares, known commonly as the X drive.

    To ensure students are able to save any files stored on the X drive, on Oct. 28, the X drive will be available through Windows Virtual Desktop as read-only to allow students to copy any files they wish to keep. ITS strongly recommends each student move their files to OneDrive before the Feb. 12 deadline.

    For more information see the following ITS support pages on Accessing Windows Virtual Desktop and Accessing Microsoft OneDrive

    Question about the change can be directed to the ITS Help Desk
